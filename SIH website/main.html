Traditional Cuisine

Handia

Traditional rice beer, a fermented drink enjoyed during festivals and celebrations.

Dhuska

Deep-fried bread made from rice and lentil batter, served with curry or chutney.

Chilka Roti

Thin, crispy flatbread made from rice flour, perfect with tribal vegetables.

Art & Craft

Dokra Art

Ancient metal casting technique creating beautiful brass figurines and decorative items.

Sohrai Painting

Traditional wall art depicting animals and nature, created during Sohrai festival.

Bamboo Craft

Eco-friendly handicrafts made from bamboo, including baskets, furniture, and decorative items.
