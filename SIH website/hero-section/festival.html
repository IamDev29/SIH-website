Festivals of Jharkhand

Jharkhand celebrates vibrant festivals that reflect its tribal traditions, spiritual devotion, and cultural heritage.

Karma Festival

One of the most popular tribal festivals celebrated with music, dance, and rituals around the sacred Karma tree.

Bol Bam

Thousands of devotees carry holy water to offer at Baba Baidyanath Temple in Deoghar during Shravan month.

Sarhul Festival

A spring festival where nature and ancestors are worshipped with flowers, dance, and community feasts.
