Art & Craft of Jharkhand

Jharkhand's art and craft reflect the vibrant traditions of its tribal communities, with beautiful paintings, intricate wood carvings, and exquisite handicrafts made using age-old techniques.

Sohrai Paintings

A traditional wall painting style using natural colors, depicting animals and nature.

Wood Carving

Famous for its fine detailing and use in making masks, doors, and tribal artifacts.

Dokra Art

An ancient metal casting craft using the lost wax technique, known for its rustic beauty.
